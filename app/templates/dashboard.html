What Survived Rigorous Testing

Military Proximity (5.47x)

UFO reports cluster near military bases far more than population alone would predict. Top bases by report proximity:

  • McChord AFB (WA): 2,176 reports
  • Travis AFB (CA): 2,138 reports
  • Point Mugu NAS (CA): 1,917 reports
  • Luke AFB (AZ): 1,545 reports

Temporal Patterns

  • Evening peak: 61.8% of reports (18:00-24:00)
  • Summer peak: 32.4% of reports (Jun-Aug)
  • Weekend elevation: 1.12x weekday rate

(Reflects human behavior patterns - when people are outside looking up)

Shape Evolution (Cultural)

Reported shapes follow cultural trends:

  • Disc: 30% (1950s) → 7% (2010s)
  • Triangle: 3% (1950s) → 9.5% (2010s)
  • Light/Fireball: 15% → 34%
What Failed Testing

Most paranormal hypotheses collapse under rigorous testing. Transparency about failures is how science works.

SPECTER Hypothesis

Claim: UFOs appear as earthquake precursors
Result: FAILED at M≥4.0 threshold (inverted to 0.62x)
Note: The "8.32x" claim was an artifact of using M≥1.0

FERRO Hypothesis

Claim: UFOs cluster in iron-rich geology
Result: Did not replicate in blind test
Note: Magnetic correlation was real but explanation failed

Physical Effects Cases
